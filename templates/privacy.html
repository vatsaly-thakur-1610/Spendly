Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and allowing you to filter and manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. No personal or financial data is shared with Google or any other third party. We do not integrate with payment processors, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
